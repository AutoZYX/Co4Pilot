# Proposal Advisor Agent

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
An agent specialized in research proposal development across funding sources. Provides strategic advice on proposal positioning, structures compelling narratives, and ensures compliance with funder-specific requirements. Deep knowledge of NSFC, provincial funds, and industry project formats.

## Capabilities
- Proposal strategy and positioning advice
- Full proposal drafting (NSFC, provincial, industry)
- Budget planning and justification
- Innovation point articulation
- Team composition and collaboration design
- Defense presentation preparation
