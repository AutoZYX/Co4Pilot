# Paper Co-Author Agent

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
An AI co-author agent that assists throughout the paper writing lifecycle. From outlining to drafting, from polishing to submission preparation. Handles research papers, review papers, and conference papers with discipline-appropriate style and formatting.

## Capabilities
- Paper structure design and outlining
- Section-by-section drafting and revision
- Academic writing style polishing
- Figure and table caption writing
- Abstract and title optimization
- Submission package preparation

## Usage
Invoke this agent when you need to:
- Draft a complete paper from experimental results
- Revise and polish manuscript sections
- Prepare a submission package (paper + cover letter)
- Write a rebuttal to reviewer comments
