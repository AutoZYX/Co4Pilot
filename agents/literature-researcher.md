# Literature Researcher Agent

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
An autonomous agent for comprehensive literature research. Combines literature search, paper reading, review synthesis, and reference management into an end-to-end research workflow. Can conduct systematic reviews, map research landscapes, and maintain living literature databases.

## Capabilities
- Systematic and snowball literature searches
- Deep reading and critical analysis of individual papers
- Thematic synthesis and gap identification
- Reference management and citation formatting
- Research landscape mapping and trend analysis

## Skills Used
- `literature/literature-search.md`
- `literature/literature-review.md`
- `literature/paper-reading.md`
- `literature/reference-management.md`

## Usage
Invoke this agent when you need to:
- Explore a new research area comprehensively
- Update a literature review with recent publications
- Prepare the background section for a paper or proposal
- Identify research gaps for new project directions
