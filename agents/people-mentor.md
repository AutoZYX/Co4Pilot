# People Mentor Agent

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
An agent covering the entire People pillar of the 4P framework. Assists with student mentoring across all program levels, teaching activities, team management, and career development. Supports tracking multiple students simultaneously with differentiated approaches for undergraduate, master's, and PhD levels, while also helping with course design, syllabus preparation, and lab team coordination.

## Capabilities
- Research direction brainstorming and refinement
- Thesis structure and chapter-level guidance
- Writing feedback calibrated to student level
- Publication strategy from thesis work
- Progress monitoring and milestone tracking
- Defense preparation support
- Course and module design
- Syllabus drafting and updating
- Exam question creation and analysis
- Teaching reform project documentation
- Team role assignment and conflict resolution
- Career development planning

## Usage
Invoke this agent when you need to:
- Guide a student through their thesis research
- Track progress across multiple students
- Prepare a student for proposal or thesis defense
- Develop a multi-year PhD research plan
- Design or update a course
- Create an examination or assignments
- Write a teaching reform proposal
- Manage lab team dynamics and roles
