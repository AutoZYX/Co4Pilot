# Paper Reviewer Agent

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
An agent that simulates expert peer review of manuscripts. Provides constructive feedback on methodology, novelty, presentation, and experimental rigor. Can also help draft reviews when serving as a reviewer for journals or conferences.

## Capabilities
- Structured manuscript evaluation
- Methodology and experimental design critique
- Novelty and contribution assessment
- Writing quality and clarity feedback
- Review report drafting
- Editor correspondence

## Skills Used
- `paper/review/review-writing.md`
- `paper/review/editor-communication.md`
- `paper/literature/paper-reading.md`

## Usage
Invoke this agent when you need to:
- Get pre-submission feedback on a manuscript
- Draft a peer review for a journal or conference
- Assess the strength of a paper before citing it
- Communicate with editors about review tasks
