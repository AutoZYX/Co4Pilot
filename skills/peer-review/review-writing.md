# Review Writing

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Draft constructive peer reviews for journal and conference submissions. Provides structured evaluation covering novelty, methodology, experiments, writing quality, and overall recommendation. Follows best practices for fair and helpful reviewing.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Write a peer review for a submitted manuscript
- Evaluate methodology and experimental rigor
- Provide constructive feedback with specific suggestions
- Determine an appropriate recommendation (accept/revise/reject)
