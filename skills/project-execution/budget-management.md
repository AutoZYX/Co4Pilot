# Budget Management

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Manage research project budgets across funding sources. Covers budget planning, expense categorization, compliance with funder rules, spending forecasts, and reallocation requests. Supports NSFC, MOST, and university-specific budget categories.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Plan a project budget across categories
- Track spending against budget allocations
- Draft budget reallocation requests
- Ensure compliance with funder financial rules
