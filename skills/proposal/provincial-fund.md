# Provincial Fund Proposal

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Draft proposals for provincial and municipal science and technology funds. Covers various provincial programs (e.g., Jilin Province S&T Development Plan, Key R&D Programs). Handles region-specific requirements and priorities.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Draft a provincial science fund proposal
- Align research with regional development priorities
- Write industry collaboration and application sections
- Prepare budget and milestone plans
