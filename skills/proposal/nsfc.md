# NSFC Proposal Writing

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Draft proposals for the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC). Covers General Program, Young Scientists Fund, Key Program, and other categories. Handles the specific format requirements, evaluation criteria, and review process of NSFC.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Draft an NSFC proposal (general, youth, key, etc.)
- Structure research significance and literature review sections
- Write research objectives, content, and technical approach
- Prepare budget justification and team introduction
