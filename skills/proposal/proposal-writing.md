# Proposal Writing (General)

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
General research proposal writing skill applicable across funding agencies. Covers problem statement, significance, methodology design, innovation points, feasibility analysis, and expected outcomes. Foundation skill used by specific fund-targeted skills.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Structure a compelling research proposal
- Write clear problem statements and research questions
- Design methodology and experimental plans
- Articulate innovation points and expected impact
