# CV Maintenance

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Maintain and update academic CVs and biosketches. Supports multiple formats (full CV, 2-page resume, NSF biosketch, NSFC resume, Google Scholar profile). Handles publication lists, grant records, student supervision records, and service activities.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Update your academic CV with new publications/grants
- Create a tailored CV for a specific purpose
- Format publication lists in various styles
- Prepare biosketches for grant applications
