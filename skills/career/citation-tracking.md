# Citation Tracking

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Track and analyze citation metrics across platforms (Google Scholar, Web of Science, Scopus, CNKI). Monitors h-index, i10-index, total citations, and individual paper performance. Supports citation alert setup and trend analysis.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Generate a citation report for annual review
- Track individual paper citation performance
- Compare metrics across databases
- Identify highly-cited papers and emerging citations
