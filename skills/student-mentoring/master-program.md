Comprehensive mentoring for master's students throughout their program. Covers coursework planning, research methodology training, thesis writing, publication strategy, and career guidance. Includes milestone tracking and committee management.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Plan a master's student research trajectory
- Guide thesis research and writing
- Develop publication plans from thesis work
- Prepare for thesis proposal and defense
