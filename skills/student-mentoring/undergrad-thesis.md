# Undergraduate Thesis Guidance

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Guide undergraduate students through their graduation thesis/design. Covers topic selection, literature review basics, methodology guidance, writing structure, and defense preparation. Calibrated for undergraduate-level expectations.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Help an undergraduate student select a thesis topic
- Guide thesis structure and writing
- Review and provide feedback on draft chapters
- Prepare students for thesis defense
