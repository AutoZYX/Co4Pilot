# Research Direction Planning

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Help students identify and refine research directions. Covers trend analysis, gap identification, feasibility assessment, and alignment with advisor expertise and lab resources. Supports brainstorming and narrowing research questions.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Help a student find a suitable research topic
- Evaluate feasibility and novelty of proposed directions
- Align research with lab capabilities and funding
- Refine broad interests into specific research questions
