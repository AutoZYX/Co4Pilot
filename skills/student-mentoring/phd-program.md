# PhD Program Mentoring

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
End-to-end PhD student mentoring across 3-5 year programs. Covers research direction, comprehensive exam preparation, proposal defense, publication strategy, dissertation writing, job market preparation, and independent researcher development.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Plan a PhD student's multi-year research agenda
- Guide dissertation research and writing
- Develop a publication strategy for the PhD
- Prepare for qualifying exams and dissertation defense
