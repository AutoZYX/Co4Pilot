# Student Progress Tracking

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Track and manage student research progress. Supports milestone planning, weekly/monthly reports, publication timelines, and graduation readiness assessment. Handles multiple students across different program levels.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Create milestone plans for student research
- Track progress across multiple students
- Generate progress reports and summaries
- Identify students who need additional support
