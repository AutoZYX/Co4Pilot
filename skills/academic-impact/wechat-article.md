# WeChat Article

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Draft WeChat Official Account articles to disseminate research findings to a broader audience. Covers accessible science writing, visual storytelling, bilingual content, and engagement optimization for the Chinese academic community.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Write a WeChat article about a new publication
- Explain research findings for a general audience
- Create bilingual (Chinese/English) research summaries
- Design engaging visual content for social media
