# Patent Mining

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Identify patentable ideas from research results and technical innovations. Analyzes novelty, inventive step, and industrial applicability. Supports prior art search across Chinese (CNIPA) and international (USPTO, EPO) patent databases.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Identify patentable innovations from research work
- Conduct prior art searches
- Assess novelty and inventive step
- Plan a patent portfolio strategy
