# Office Action Response

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Draft responses to patent office actions (OA). Handles rejections based on novelty (102), inventive step (103), clarity (112), and other grounds. Supports claim amendments, arguments, and declarations.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Respond to patent office rejections
- Amend patent claims to overcome prior art
- Draft arguments distinguishing over cited references
- Prepare declarations or affidavits
