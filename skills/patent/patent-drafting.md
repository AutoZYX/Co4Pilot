# Patent Drafting

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Draft patent applications including claims, specification, abstract, and drawings descriptions. Supports Chinese invention patents, utility models, and PCT international applications. Follows CNIPA and USPTO formatting requirements.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Draft patent claims (independent and dependent)
- Write detailed specification with embodiments
- Prepare patent abstracts and drawing descriptions
- Structure applications for Chinese or international filing
