# Exam Design

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Design examinations and assessments for university courses. Covers question writing (multiple choice, short answer, essay, programming), rubric design, difficulty balancing, and exam analysis. Supports both formative and summative assessments.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Design midterm or final examinations
- Create assignment rubrics
- Balance question difficulty and coverage
- Analyze exam results and item statistics
