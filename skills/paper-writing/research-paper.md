# Research Paper Writing

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
End-to-end research paper drafting skill. Supports structuring, writing, and polishing original research manuscripts for journals and conferences. Handles IMRaD format, academic English/Chinese, and discipline-specific conventions.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Draft a complete research paper from outline to final manuscript
- Structure arguments and present results effectively
- Write specific sections (abstract, introduction, methods, results, discussion)
- Polish academic writing style and language
