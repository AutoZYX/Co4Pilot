# Paper Reading

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Deep reading and analysis of individual research papers. Extracts key contributions, methodology details, experimental setup, results, and limitations. Supports structured note-taking and critical assessment.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to:
- Critically analyze a specific paper
- Extract methodology and experimental details
- Summarize key findings and contributions
- Identify strengths, weaknesses, and open questions
