# PhD Journey Workflow

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Multi-year workflow spanning the entire PhD program from enrollment to graduation. Orchestrates research direction, coursework, publications, dissertation, and career preparation across 3-5 years.

## Workflow Stages

### Year 1: Foundation
- **Agent**: `thesis-supervisor` + `literature-researcher`
- **Activities**: Coursework planning, research direction exploration, initial literature survey, first project identification
- **Milestones**: Course completion, research area defined, first project started

### Year 2: Development
- **Agent**: `thesis-supervisor` + `paper-coauthor`
- **Activities**: Deep research, first publication(s), qualifying exam preparation, proposal development
- **Milestones**: First publication, qualifying exam passed, thesis proposal approved

### Year 3: Production
- **Agent**: `paper-coauthor` + `patent-strategist`
- **Activities**: Core research execution, multiple publications, patent filings, conference presentations
- **Milestones**: 2-3 publications, patent applications, international conference participation

### Year 4: Synthesis (if applicable)
- **Agent**: `thesis-supervisor` + `paper-coauthor`
- **Activities**: Dissertation writing, remaining publications, thesis committee meetings
- **Milestones**: Dissertation draft complete, all required publications submitted

### Final Stage: Graduation
- **Agent**: `thesis-supervisor` + `impact-strategist`
- **Activities**: Dissertation defense preparation, career planning, job market materials
- **Milestones**: Successful defense, dissertation submitted, career transition
