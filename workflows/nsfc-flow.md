# NSFC Proposal Workflow

> Status: Under Development

## Overview
Complete workflow for NSFC (National Natural Science Foundation of China) proposal development, from topic identification through submission and post-award management. Covers General Program, Young Scientists Fund, and Key Program categories.

## Workflow Stages

### Stage 1: Topic & Funding Identification (6-8 months before deadline)
- **Agent**: `proposal-advisor` + `literature-researcher`
- **Activities**: Identify funding opportunities, assess fit, review funded projects
- **Deliverable**: Target fund category and preliminary topic

### Stage 2: Literature & Landscape Analysis (4-6 months before)
- **Agent**: `literature-researcher`
- **Activities**: Comprehensive literature review, identify gaps, map competitors
- **Deliverable**: Literature review and research landscape analysis

### Stage 3: Proposal Drafting (2-4 months before)
- **Agent**: `proposal-advisor`
- **Activities**: Draft all sections, articulate innovation, plan methodology
- **Deliverable**: Complete proposal draft

### Stage 4: Budget & Team (1-2 months before)
- **Agent**: `proposal-advisor` + `project-manager`
- **Activities**: Budget planning, team composition, collaboration letters
- **Deliverable**: Budget justification and team documentation

### Stage 5: Review & Polish (2-4 weeks before)
- **Agent**: `proposal-advisor` + `academic-writer`
- **Activities**: Internal review, language polishing, formatting check
- **Deliverable**: Final polished proposal

### Stage 6: Defense Preparation (if shortlisted)
- **Agent**: `proposal-advisor`
- **Activities**: Prepare presentation, anticipate questions, rehearse
- **Deliverable**: Defense slides and Q&A preparation

### Stage 7: Post-Award Management
- **Agent**: `project-manager`
- **Activities**: Kickoff planning, milestone tracking, reporting
- **Deliverable**: Project management plan
